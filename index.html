TYPES OF CLASSES

Gentle Flow

Gentler practice, less intense positions, and usually includes extended time for meditation, yogic breath work, and relaxation

Vinyasa Yoga

Also called "flow" because of the smooth way the poses run together, is one of the most popular contemporary styles of yoga.

Hatha Yoga

 A branch of yoga which uses physical techniques to try to preserve and channel the vital force or energy. This yoga is about bringing balance.

Power Yoga

A fast-paced style of yoga that's focused on building strength and endurance. It is also an excellent form of yoga for burning calories.

My Story

How I started my journey

In High School, I was in Cheerleading, and I participated in  many International competitions. As I starting to grow, I realized the pressure and competitiveness it came with it. I also started to deal with various stressful situations in my life, and ventured into yoga. There is no competition in yoga, there is no right or wrong time, you just simply grow, and connect with yourself. You achieve peace.
